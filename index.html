What does a bazaar look like in practice?

  • Porous organizational boundaries
  • Encourage initiatives & minimalize friction
  • Decentralized governance and turned on those who make
  • Few rules, but deeply shared (culture > rules)
  • Dissemination of information (transparency)
  • Diversions and modifications
  • Strong sense of belonging
  • No spectators, only participants
